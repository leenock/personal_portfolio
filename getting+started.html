Things I need to get Started

September 30, 2019

Sometimes we can end up working on multiple projects at the sametime and we can happen to have some difficult getting started whenever a new projects comes along.But when we began making progress you can be able to move well without any problems. Something that doesnt interest you can contribute to the delay of getting started.At intellectual level you should know that you have to get started , but you are not able to summon up the motivation to begin.

Some of the technique that can help you out to get started is changing the environement you are used to before and maybe by looking for an open place you are not used to or coffee shop or visiting hub around can help you get that motive of getting started and ofcourse if its a coffee shop dont forget to buy a cup of coffee , it does help out alot.

Always Seek out someone who has already achieved the outcome.Whatever it is you are procrastinating on, if you want to get it done, you need to get a grip on yourself and do it.

Procrastinating behaviour is among the factors any person should avoid when planning to get started on something. some of the practical ways for procrastinators to help them stop Procrastinating can be, breaking your work into little steps to avoid the feeling of having too much work to accomplish or finish. Through this you can focus on one part at a time.Change of environment like i said can impact your productivity.Look at your work desk or room, do they make you want to snuggle and sleep?. Creating a detailed timeline with specific deadlines , breakdown your project then create an overall timeline with specific deadlines for each small task

Hanging out with people who inspire you to take action , the 10 minutes you take to talk to someone that inspires you ,you will be more inspired than spending the 10 minutes doing nuthing. Getting a buddy makes the whole process much more fun , ideally your buddy should be someone who has his or her own set of goals. Tell others about your goals and whenever you see them, they are bound to ask you about your status on those projects.
